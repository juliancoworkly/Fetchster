# Privacy Policy - Fetchster

**Last Updated: May 28, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Fetchster ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our business intelligence platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide:
- **Account Information**: Email address, full name, password (encrypted)
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store card details)
- **Communication Data**: Support requests, feedback, and correspondence

### Automatically Collected Information:
- **Usage Data**: Search queries, search results, platform usage patterns
- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, device information, session data
- **Analytics Data**: Page views, feature usage, performance metrics

### Search and Business Data:
- **Search Queries**: Keywords, locations, and search parameters you enter
- **Search Results**: Business contact information and email addresses discovered
- **Search History**: Record of your searches for service improvement and billing

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### Service Provision:
- Process and fulfill your search requests
- Maintain and improve platform functionality
- Provide customer support and technical assistance
- Process payments and manage subscriptions

### Business Operations:
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our service
- Monitor for fraud, abuse, and security threats
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
- Communicate important service updates

### Marketing (With Consent):
- Send product updates and feature announcements
- Provide educational content about lead generation
- Offer promotional pricing and special offers

## 4. Data Sources and Third-Party Services

### Search Data Sources:
- **Google Search & Maps**: Public business listings and websites
- **Serper.dev API**: Search results aggregation service
- **Public Websites**: Publicly available business contact information

### Service Providers:
- **Supabase**: Secure database hosting and user authentication
- **Stripe**: Payment processing and subscription management
- **Replit**: Application hosting and deployment

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do Not Sell Your Data
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Limited Sharing Circumstances:
- **Service Providers**: Trusted partners who help operate our platform
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers**: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale
- **Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing

### Data Processing Locations:
- Primary servers located in secure data centers
- Data may be processed in the United States and European Union
- All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws

## 6. Data Security

### Technical Safeguards:
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
- **Monitoring**: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
- **Compliance**: SOC 2, ISO 27001, and PCI DSS compliance standards

### Administrative Safeguards:
- Regular security training for all personnel
- Background checks for employees with data access
- Incident response procedures and breach notification protocols

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control:
- **Account Access**: View and update your profile information
- **Data Portability**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate personal information

### Communication Preferences:
- **Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
- **Preferences**: Manage notification settings in your account
- **Do Not Track**: We respect browser do-not-track signals

### Regional Rights (GDPR, CCPA):
- **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect and how we use it
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Correct**: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
- **Right to Opt-out**: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)

## 8. Data Retention

### Account Data:
- Retained while your account is active
- Deleted within 30 days of account closure (unless legally required)

### Search History:
- Retained for service improvement and billing verification
- Anonymized after 24 months for analytics purposes

### Payment Records:
- Retained as required by law and payment processors
- Financial records kept for 7 years for tax and legal compliance

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

### Essential Cookies:
- Authentication and session management
- Security and fraud prevention
- Basic functionality and user preferences

### Analytics Cookies:
- Usage patterns and feature popularity
- Performance monitoring and optimization
- Error tracking and debugging

### Cookie Management:
- Browser settings to control cookie preferences
- Opt-out options for non-essential tracking
- Clear information about cookie purposes

## 10. Children's Privacy

Fetchster is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

## 11. International Data Transfers

For users outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws. We ensure adequate protection through:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
- Other approved transfer mechanisms
